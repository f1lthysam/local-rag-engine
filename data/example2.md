# About Alian AI

Engineered in India. Shipping AI globally.

Alian AI is the AI services division of Alian Software, a 15+ year old engineering firm shipping production enterprise systems for clients across manufacturing, finance, and SaaS.

The AI division builds production-ready AI that ships in weeks, backed by people who have shipped real software for a long time. The team builds chatbots, agents, RAG apps, automation pipelines, and predictive models, then hands them over to the client's team to run.

## Company facts

- 15+ years of Alian Software engineering DNA
- 4 service pillars: agents, apps, strategy, and data
- Starting hourly rate: $25
- Typical fixed-fee sprint timeline: 4-8 weeks

## How we work

### We build, we don't pitch

Every recommendation can also be implemented by the team, which keeps scoping honest and timelines real.

### Senior engineers on every project

No juniors learning on your dime. The AI team is backed by Alian Software's 15+ years of enterprise engineering bench.

### You own the code, day one

Code, prompts, agent configs, and deployment artifacts are transferred from commit one.

### Production-ready, not slide-ready

Most engagements ship in 4-8 weeks. The team measures success by what is running in the client's stack.
